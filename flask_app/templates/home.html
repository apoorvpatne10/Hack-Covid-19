Hack Covid 19 - Portal

This is our demonstration for CodeDown Hackathon!

Services

A complete built web portal which will provide services to people as well as the government agencies

X-Ray Image Classification

Healthcare Image Classifier to detect the chances of COVID with the help of X-Ray images of the patient. Patient puts the X-Ray image and the portal will tell the probability to have COVID-19.

Food Supply Management

Food Supplies Token based purchase of food supplies to avoid unnecessary buying. Government supply vehicles will give home delivery based on the tokens generated by people. Multiple tokens will not be issued to the user in span of 1 week so that everyone can be benefitted.

Contact Tracing

Use of Google Timeline services to track the location where the patient has travelled in the last 14 days. With this data other people who might have came in contact can be traced. Google Timeline will be used to get the location where the patient had travelled in the past 10-14 days.

Fake News Identification

Fake news circulating in social media. People feeds in images or text of suspected Fake News and our portal tells whether its fake or not . Webscraping of popular authentic websites like- WHO,Health department of India/State,worldometer.org etc will be done to check the authenticity of the news.
